Why a domain like this needs rescuing

Type "fast money" into any search engine, in almost any country, and you'll find the same thing: promises of make money online schemes, get-rich-quick crypto pitches, recycled MLM decks, and "passive income" courses that mostly generate income for the person selling the course. Domain names built around money and speed have earned that reputation honestly — because for years, that's almost all they've been used for.

And yet the underlying need is completely real. People everywhere — from Lagos to Manila to São Paulo to small towns in the American Midwest — are genuinely searching for legit ways to make money online, a side hustle that actually pays, work from home jobs that aren't fronts for something else, or just a clearer path to financial freedom for their family. That search is honest. Most of what it leads to is not.

BuyBuild.xyz believes a domain's reputation isn't fixed — it's built, one honest product at a time. So instead of listing fastmoney.one for sale to the highest bidder (almost certainly another get-rich-quick operation), BuyBuild is running an experiment: find one person with a real idea, give them the domain outright, fund them with $5,000, and back them publicly through launch.

If it works, fastmoney.one becomes proof that a name doesn't have to determine its outcome — that earning extra income, building a legitimate online business, or helping others do the same can be done in the open, without a single dark pattern.

No. This domain is currently unused and being offered, transparently and at no cost, to one selected applicant by BuyBuild.xyz, along with a $5,000 fund. Nothing is sold on this page and applicants are never asked for payment. The entire process is documented in public.

Who is BuyBuild.xyz?

BuyBuild.xyz runs small, public experiments that hand real assets — domains, funding, and support — to builders with honest ideas. This page is one of those experiments: reclaiming a domain that would otherwise end up powering a get-rich-quick scheme.

Can I apply from anywhere in the world?

Yes. This is open globally. Ideas are judged on merit and honesty, not geography, nationality, or background.

What kind of idea are you looking for?

Anything that genuinely helps people make, save, or manage money honestly — financial literacy tools, transparent income directories, ethical marketplaces, budgeting help, ways to find real remote work. It just has to deserve the trust the domain name asks for.

Do I need a company or working product already?

No. A clear, honest idea and a credible plan to execute it are enough to apply. The fund and support exist specifically to help you go from idea to launch.

Is there a deadline?

Every application is read personally by BuyBuild.xyz. Your information is used only to evaluate your application and to contact you about it. It is never sold or shared.
